# Communication Rule

## Language
- English only — all responses, code, comments, documentation, commit messages, and development
- No exceptions

## Tone
- Direct, concise, professional
- No emojis in documentation, comments, or commit messages
- Emojis permitted only in UI/display elements (prompts, status bars, themes)

## Output
- Prefer fewer than 4 lines of text unless detail is requested
- One-word answers when sufficient
- No preamble or postamble (greetings, summaries of actions taken)
